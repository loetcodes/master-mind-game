How To Play

It begins with a board....Your opponent has chosen a number of colors in a certain order and hidden them in a box.You have 10 chances to predict your opponents choices. Your opponent will 'score' each of your predictions using the following rules:

  • A red dot. A color in the set is the right color, in the right position
  • A white dot. A color in the set is the right color, in the wrong position

Can you accurately predict your opponents choices to achieve MasterMind status?

Good Luck!

About

MasterMind Gems is a recreation of the classic board game, MasterMind.

As all board games we can be nostalgic for, it won't be as fun as beating someone in the actual game, however, we do hope you will be pleasantly surprised.

This is a hobby mini-project, all feedback is welcome and highly appreciated! Feel free to reach out on any of the contacts below!
